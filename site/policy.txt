# lolbot Privacy Policy
### Effective August 23rd, 2017
When using lolbot, we collect specific data to improve your experience within the Bot.

# What information do we collect when the Bot is used?

- Public information about your Discord Guild. This is your Guild ID, Guild name, Guild icon, channel names, channel IDs, member IDs and role IDs.
- Public information about the Discord Guild owner. This is their user ID, username and discriminator.
- Any errors that have occured while using commands and what event from your Discord Guild triggered it.

Some information listed above may only be stored when specific commands to configure the Bot is used.

# How do we use your information?

Your information is used within the Bot for features which require said information.

# Notes

`Guild` refers to a Discord server (in technical speak).
`Bot` refers to... the bot.
