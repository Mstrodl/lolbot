Usage

Usage

You probably came here because you were wondering how to use lolbot. In that case, it's easy!
Just say ^help and the bot will say a list of commands in the chat.
The prefix for all commands is ^ as well. (Example: ^cat brings up a cat)
TL;DR: ^help
